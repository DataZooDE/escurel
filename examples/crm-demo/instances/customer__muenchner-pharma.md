# Münchner Pharma AG

Listed mid-cap pharmaceutical company headquartered in Munich.
Production sites in Bayern and Sachsen. Long-standing internal data
platform (mostly SAP HANA + Snowflake) hitting growing pains around
tenant isolation for their late-stage R&D groups.

## Why they matter

MP's R&D org is restructuring around clinical-trial-as-a-product
units, each demanding their own isolated analytical sandbox without
the procurement overhead of a separate Snowflake account. That's the
ICP fit signal that brought them to our booth.
